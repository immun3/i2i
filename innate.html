Innate Immunity

What it is?

Innate immunity is our body's default protocol when things go wrong.
To be innate is akin to being inherent, native, even essential.
Indeed, the innate immune system can be found in almost every animal;
a testament to its foundational nature and evolutionary seniority.

What it do?

Innate immunity is really so much more than just a handful of interesting
cells. It represents many of the clinical and characteristic symptoms of health.
