What is this?

This website offers a discussion into the fascinating human immune system.

Intended audience: graduate/undergraduate students studying immunology.

Created and maintained by: Pedro L. Tirado (pedrotirado@ufl.edu).

Why?

A simple Google search shows a lack of free, online immunology learning materials. Compare this to the wealth of online content available for subjects like organic chemistry or human physiology. Most immunology content is restricted to complex research publications or barred behind a paywall.

Public misinformation towards basic immunological concepts surrounding the COVID-19 pandemic reinforce the importance of understanding and educating others on topics including: pathogenesis, immunological memory (e.g., booster shots), mRNA vs. attenuated vaccines, neutralizing antibodies, and more.
