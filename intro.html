What is Immunology?

Immunology is the science of immune responses.
It's essentially the wonderchild of the union between microbiology & physiology.
The most pragmatic definition: "Immunology is the science of protein-protein
interactions." ~ Dr. J. Larkin PhD

Terminology

Unfortunately, immunology is full of complex, redundant, lengthy vocabulary.
For instance, while there's only one immune system, it goes by many names:
innate/inherent/nonspecific immunity, adaptive/acquired immunity,
humoral/antibody-mediated immunity, cellular/cell-mediated immunity, active
immunity, passive immunity, autoimmunity, diplomatic immunity...
