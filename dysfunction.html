Immune Dysfunction

Dysfunctional couples: Innate & Adaptive Immunity

The immune system does many things, so it's easy to forget
the two most important functions: accelerating & braking.

A healthy, robust immune response can quickly spring to action
in times of crisis, but also knows when to pump the brakes.
Think of it as an automobile: nobody wants a car that takes
forever to speed up. Even more dangerous is a runaway vehicle
that refuses to slow down.
